Our Story

Rennel Tech Inc. is a women owned small business which provides Management Consulting and IT Services.Registered under Women-Owned Small Business Federal Contracting Program (SBA 8A) We specialize in formulating IT Strategy, providing Advisory Services, and devising Cloud & Enterprise Architecture Strategy, and we deliver results without the overhead of large business competitors.

With a structured approach and method, we help our clients develop, implement and integrate mission-critical ERP, CRM, LMS, PPM and BI applications and processes. We are currently helping our clients develop, implement and integrate cutting edge solutions using Oracle E-Business Suite R12, Salesforce Sales & Service Cloud, Google Apps Engine & Search Appliance, SABA Cloud, Clarity, Business Intelligence, Mobile Applications and Automatic Call Distribution systems, paired with proficiency in managing enterprise PMO.
